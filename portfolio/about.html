Before Korey could talk, he could play Street Fighter II for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System. And he could beat his dear old Dad, spamming jump and A on the gamepad (sorry for spamming, Dad...).

Korey's love for games fueled his curiosity about the magic, electric boxes that showed his favorite pixel heroes, like MegaMan. He's since graduated with a Bachelor's in Eletrical & Computer Engineering Technologies from Purdue North Central in 2017.

Korey plans on graduating GVSU with a Bachelor's in Writing from Grand Valley State University with hopes of researching video game related phenomenon, writing for content sites on the Web, or technical writing for technology.
