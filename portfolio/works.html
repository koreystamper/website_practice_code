Game Studies Research

In 2022, Korey researched Private Servers in online games. A private server is an online game server that is a divergent experiece of an already existing game. Korey noticed that as millions of players were leaving World of Warcraft (WoW) from 2011 to 2017, there were hundreds of thousands playing rogue servers, like Nostalria.

In 2016, Blizzard Entertainment sent a cease and desist order to the developer of Nostalria. Over 800,000 accounts-- out of nowhere-- could no longer enjoy the classic WoW experience that Nostalria players did previously. Instead of working with the developer, Blizzard chose to shut him out.

Cases like this allow researchers to draw parallels to other private server case studies in hopes of finding the reasons why folks love to play private servers. If you have data or information regarding a private server, reach out to me via my contact page.

In 2023, Korey is developing a research project around the reward systems in MMORPGs.
